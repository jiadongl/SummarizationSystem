The Christian Science Monitor  newspaper on Monday pleaded for the release of American reporter  Jill Carroll, seized in Baghdad by abductors who gunned down her  Iraqi translator.
The family of Jill  Carroll, a US woman journalist abducted in Iraq, issued an emotional  appeal Monday for her release after her captors threatened her with  execution.
The Christian Science Monitor on  Monday named a US journalist kidnapped in Iraq as freelancer Jill  Carroll, who was on assignment for the US-based paper when she was  seized.
